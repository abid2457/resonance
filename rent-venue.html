Jio Garden World

Jio World Garden is a premier open-air turfed venue located in Mumbai's Bandra Kurla Complex (BKC). Spanning a total area of 37,252 square meters, with a 13,105 square meter turf, it can accommodate up to 9,000 guests and offers parking for 2,000 vehicles. The garden's design features stunning landscapes, soothing water fountains, tranquil lotus ponds, seating areas, and energy-generating air trees, creating an ideal setting for cultural, community, and sporting events The venue offers multiple entry and exit points, spacious elevators, and is fully accessible to guests with special needs. Amenities include multiple drinking water fountains, well-maintained public conveniences, and venue-wide 4G connectivity. Security measures such as 24/7 CCTV surveillance, fire detection systems, and emergency assistance are in place to ensure visitor safety. For those interested in hosting events, Jio World Garden provides a dynamic space suitable for social gatherings, cultural events, sports and wellness activities, and community events. The Events Operations Team offers end-to-end support, from planning to execution. Located in the heart of Mumbai, Jio World Garden is easily accessible by car, bus, local train, and the upcoming metro lines 2 and 3.
